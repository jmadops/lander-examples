## Copy Patterns

- **Headline formula:** Number + Benefit + Trending Claim — "11 Reasons Why This High-Protein Iced Coffee is the #1 Trending Drink for 2026"
- **Body copy structure:** Short paragraphs per reason (2-4 sentences each). Conversational, benefit-focused tone. Each reason has a clear numbered heading, brief explanation, and soft CTA link. British English spellings (flavoured, favourite) suggesting UK-targeted audience.
- **Claims presentation:** Bold for key stats and differentiators ("real 100% arabica coffee!", "average person spends £2,400+"). Italic for emphasis statements. Emoji-prefixed category labels add visual scanning hooks.
- **Social proof patterns:** "100 MILLION coffees" community stat, "30-Day Money-Back Guarantee" risk reversal, author byline with photo for editorial credibility, "Verified Lowest Price Ever" badge
- **Urgency/scarcity tactics:** Countdown timer (sticky bar + offer section), "limited-time deal in high demand," "stock keeps selling out," "Sell-Out Risk: High," seasonal sale framing (Valentine's Day)
- **Emoji usage patterns:** Heavy emoji usage throughout — each reason category has a unique emoji (☕🔒🌿🚫🤑🎯⚡💪👍🌍), 👉 for soft CTAs, 💘✨ for seasonal, 🎁 for free gifts, 🏷️ for sale. Clean integration with text, not excessive.
